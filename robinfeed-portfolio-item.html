    What is Robinfeed?

    Robinfeed is vertically integrated food delivery system that takes in what would be wasted food from grocery stores and turns them into sustainable and deliverable meals for students on campus.

    What did I do?

    I was the solo founder of Robinfeed

    Conducted market research to understand the habits and needs of students with food insecurity through surveys and user interviews

    What happened as a result?

    I performed a proof of concept in order to build out the demand side of my MVP
